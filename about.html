Who is Suraj Kumar Chandravanshi?

Suraj Kumar Chandravanshi is a Delhi-based SEO and Digital Marketing Specialist with over four years of hands-on professional experience. He helps B2B and B2C brands improve their online visibility, generate qualified leads, and grow revenue through data-driven digital marketing strategies.

He specialises in organic search engine optimisation (SEO), Google Ads campaign management, Meta advertising (Facebook and Instagram), social media strategy, WordPress content publishing, and marketing performance reporting. He has managed digital marketing for more than ten client websites across a range of industries.

Professional Experience

Suraj worked as an SEO Executive and Social Media Manager at AT Digital Work Agency in Delhi from October 2020 to January 2024, where he managed SEO campaigns, paid advertising, social media accounts, and client reporting for multiple B2B and B2C clients simultaneously.

Alongside his agency work, he co-founded an independent business venture from 2021 to 2023, where he was responsible for client acquisition, digital strategy, and end-to-end delivery of SEO and paid advertising services for small and medium-sized businesses.

  • Social Media Marketing: Content calendars, account management, brand engagement, and organic growth strategies across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook.
  • Content Marketing: SEO blog writing, WordPress publishing, Canva visuals, and conversion-focused copywriting.
  • Analytics and Reporting: Google Analytics, Google Search Console, SEMrush, Ahrefs, and Google Sheets dashboards.
