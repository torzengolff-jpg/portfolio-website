Case Studies

SEO Growth

E-commerce Brand — Organic Traffic Growth

3× increase in organic sessions over 6 months

Problem: An e-commerce client had strong products but almost no organic search presence — over 90% of traffic came from paid sources.

Approach: Full technical SEO audit, keyword clustering by intent, on-page optimisation across 40+ product pages, and a structured link-building outreach programme targeting industry blogs.

  • Organic sessions grew 3× within 6 months
  • Target keywords moved from page 4 to page 1
  • Organic share of total traffic rose from 8% to 34%
Paid Media

B2B Services Company — Google Ads & Meta Campaigns

40% reduction in cost-per-lead vs. previous campaigns

Problem: A B2B services client was running Google Ads with no clear conversion tracking — spending budget without knowing which campaigns generated leads.

Approach: Rebuilt campaign structure from scratch, set up Google Tag Manager conversion tracking, split-tested 3 audience segments on Meta, and introduced weekly performance reporting.

  • Cost-per-lead reduced by 40% in 90 days
  • Conversion tracking implemented end-to-end
  • Meta retargeting added second lead source
